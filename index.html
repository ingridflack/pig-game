How to play

1 - Choose between Player 1 or Player 2
2 - Click on "ROLL DICE" to add points
3 - If dice scores 1, your current score will be reset
4 - To add the current score to main score, click on "HOLD"
5 - The player that scores 100 points, will be the winner
6 - To play again, click on "NEW GAME"
